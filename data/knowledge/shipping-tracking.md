# Shipping and Tracking

## Shipping Methods
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days, free on orders over $50
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days, $9.99
- **Overnight Shipping**: Next business day, $19.99

## Tracking Your Order
1. Log into your TechGear account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Click on the order number to see tracking details
4. You can also use the tracking number directly on the carrier's website

## Common Tracking Statuses
- **Processing**: Order received, being prepared for shipment
- **Shipped**: Package has left our warehouse
- **In Transit**: Package is with the carrier and on its way
- **Out for Delivery**: Package will arrive today
- **Delivered**: Package has been delivered

## Shipping Delays
If your package is delayed:
- Standard shipping: Wait up to 10 business days before contacting support
- Express shipping: Contact support after 4 business days
- Check for weather or carrier service alerts in your area

## International Shipping
We currently ship to US addresses only. International shipping is coming soon.
