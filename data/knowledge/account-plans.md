# Account and Subscription Plans

## Plans

### Free Plan
- Standard shipping rates
- 30-day return window
- Email support (24-48 hour response)
- 1-year product warranty

### Premium Plan ($9.99/month)
- Free standard shipping on all orders
- 60-day return window
- Priority support (4-hour response)
- 2-year extended warranty on all products
- Exclusive early access to new products
- 10% discount on accessories

## Account Management
- To upgrade your plan, go to Account > Subscription
- Plan changes take effect immediately
- Downgrading preserves your benefits until the end of the billing period
- You can cancel your premium plan at any time

## Password Reset
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for the reset link (valid for 24 hours)
5. If you don't receive it, check spam or contact support

## Data Privacy
We take your privacy seriously. You can:
- Download your data: Account > Privacy > Export Data
- Delete your account: Account > Privacy > Delete Account (irreversible)
- Manage marketing preferences: Account > Notifications
