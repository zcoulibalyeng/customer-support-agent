# Product Troubleshooting

## Wireless Headphones Pro
**Not charging:**
1. Try a different USB-C cable
2. Clean the charging port with compressed air
3. Hold the power button for 15 seconds to hard reset
4. If still not working, contact support for warranty replacement

**Bluetooth not connecting:**
1. Turn off Bluetooth on your device, then turn it back on
2. Remove the headphones from your device's Bluetooth list
3. Put headphones in pairing mode (hold power button 5 seconds until blue light flashes)
4. Search for "TG-WHP" in your Bluetooth settings

**Poor audio quality:**
1. Ensure headphones are fully charged
2. Move closer to your connected device
3. Check that the audio codec is set to AAC or aptX in your device settings
4. Update headphone firmware via the TechGear app

## Smart Watch Basic
**Screen not responding:**
1. Force restart by holding the side button for 20 seconds
2. Ensure the watch is charged above 10%
3. Check for software updates in the TechGear app

**Not tracking steps:**
1. Ensure the watch is worn snugly on your wrist
2. Restart the watch
3. Recalibrate in Settings > Health > Calibrate

## Mechanical Keyboard
**Keys not registering:**
1. Try a different USB port
2. Check if the issue persists in a text editor (might be app-specific)
3. Remove and reseat the keycap
4. Test with the keyboard's built-in tester (Fn + F12)
