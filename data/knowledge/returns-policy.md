# Returns and Refunds Policy

## Return Window
All TechGear products can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
Products must be in original packaging and in resellable condition.

## Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defective product.

## Damaged or Defective Products
If you receive a damaged or defective product:
1. Contact support within 48 hours of delivery
2. Provide photos of the damage
3. We will issue a prepaid return label
4. Full refund including shipping will be processed

## Premium Plan Members
Premium plan members receive:
- Extended 60-day return window
- Free return shipping on all orders
- Priority refund processing (1-2 business days)

## Non-Returnable Items
- Personalized/custom products
- Products with broken seals (hygiene items)
- Software licenses after activation
